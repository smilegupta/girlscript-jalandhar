About Us

Girlscript Jalandhar is a non-profit Technical Community under Girlscript Foundation

GirlScript Foundation is a section-8 NON PROFIT company registered under the government of India on 2nd June 2017. GirlScript started as a project has been selected by Mozilla under Open Leadership program out of 51 open-source projects worldwide. GirlScript hosts many tech events and coding competition to help you get started and learn computer programming.

Girlscript Jalandhar is an emerging developer community in India which has been shaping around in order to enhance the sharing of knowledge and saddling the scope of learning strategy in Innovation and Technology which endeavors to bolster and make champions in education out of their users by different cross-utilitarian learnings.

NETWORKING

Networking supports the fellow peers for taking part in our online programs and workshops.Sharing of technical knowledge and content for helping the individuals no matter experienced or a beginner in the world of Technology

SKILL DEVELOPMENT

We as a community provide a platform to individuals to learn different kind of skills by hosting events , workshops, meetups and various other exercises by which we are able to offer assistance for career improvement and professional development

EMPOWERMENT

We encourage people to learn basics to advanced concepts. We empower them by providing them a platform to provide them an opportunity to showcase their technical skills.

OUR MISSION

Girlscript Foundation lays down a strong mission to empower people especially the under represented groups by educating them over technology and other necessary skills for their personal and career development.

OUR GOAL

We are strongly determined to educate people by introducing the tech content that is helpful for everyone, and by spreding the awareness about variuos opportunities in our city Jalandhar.
